TF-IDF Vectorizer

Converts text into numerical features by weighting term frequency against inverse document frequency across 30,000 vocabulary terms with bigrams.

🧠

Naive Bayes

Multinomial Naive Bayes assumes feature independence and applies Bayes' theorem — extremely fast and effective for high-dimensional sparse text data.

⚡

Why It Works

Despite the "naive" independence assumption, NB excels at text classification because word co-occurrence patterns in TF-IDF space are highly discriminative per class.
